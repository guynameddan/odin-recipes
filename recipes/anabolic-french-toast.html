Anabolic French Toast

Description

Made internet famous by Greg Doucette. This delicious breakfast is low in calories and filling.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup egg whites
  • 4 slices of any bread
  • 1 packet of sweetner
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix all ingredients except bread.
  2. Heat a griddle.
  3. Dip bread in the egg white mixture.
  4. Cook both sides.
  5. Remove from griddle. Top with low calorie toppings like fruit and non-fat greek yogurt. Enjoy!
