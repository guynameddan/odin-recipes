Instant Pot Greek Yogurt

Description

Ever look for something low in calories and high in protein? Look no further. Non-fat greek yogurt is here! It's easy to make and cheap.

Ingredients

  • non-fat milk (any milk will do)
  • yogurt

Steps

  1. Pour milk into instant pot and hit the yogurt button until it says boil.
  2. After the instant pot beeps, take out the inner pot and let it cool to room temp.
  3. There may be things floating on top depending on what milk was used. Remove them.
  4. Mix in the yogurt.
  5. Place the innner pot back into the instant pot and hit the yogurt button until 8 hours is shown.
  6. Let the mixture incubate until the timer ends.
  7. Strain the yogurt of all the fluids then store in fridge and enjoy whenever.
